Q. What is Bus Go?

Ans.- Bus go is an web application which is use for tracking the local city buses by the help of GPS system .

Q.How to use Bus Go?

Ans.-It is very simple to use. Here are the following steps: -
  1. In the INDEX page where two boxes are given.Type -From where you want to take the bus. Type
    To-where you want to go then click go
  2. Then the map page will open. Then all bus location will be shown in the map that is nearby you.
  3. Then you can see the details of all the buses
  5. If you want to see the details or schedules of the buses the there is a timetable option in the option button.
  6. when you go on the timetable page you will be able to know the routes of all the buses and fares.
  7. you can also purchase tickets or passes of the bus .
  8. Then there is a feedback form your complain and for your suggestions.
  9. And there is a Panic button for all the persons who are in emergency.
